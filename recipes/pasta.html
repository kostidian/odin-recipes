Pasta Dough

Description

This is a basic recipe for making pasta dough that can be rolled out and formed into many different shapes and sizes.

Ingredients

  • 400g 00 Flour
  • 4 eggs

Method

  1. Tip pasta onto a clean working bench and make a well in the middle
  2. Crack eggs into the well and gradually combine with a fork
  3. Once the mixture gets tough to use a fork with, use hands to kneed the ingredients into a ball of dough.
  4. Kneed for at least 10 minutes until the dough springs back to the touch and feels smooth.
  5. Mould into a flat shape, cover in glad wrap and store in fridge until needed

Chicken Pie

Description

Made this awesome pie on the weekend. It's a must cook. Super tasty. 100% would cook again. Much chicken. Wow!

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • chicken
  • carrots
  • Celery
  • Flour
  • Chicken stock
  • Frozen peas
  • Special pastry

Method

  1. Cook onion and chicken for 10 minutes in oil
  2. Add carrots and celery and continue cooking for 5 mins
  3. Add flour and stock and stir until desired thickness is achieved
  4. Stir in frozen peas and top with special pastry
  5. Bake in 200 degree oven for 40 mins. Enjoy!

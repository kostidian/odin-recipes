Brocolli Beef

Description

This is tomorrow night's dinner, was going to make this tonight but can't be bothered. Will have leftovers instead! Yum.

Ingredients

  • Beef steak - sliced
  • Head of brocolli
  • Vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Knob of ginger, grated or sliced
  • Special sauce!
  • Rice for serving
  • Spring onions for garnish

Method

  1. Cook beef in frying pan with oil and set aside
  2. In same pan, add oil and brocolli and stir fry for 2-3 mins. Remove from pan.
  3. Add some more oil to pan and saute garlic, ginger and special sauce
  4. Combine beef, brocolli and sauce in the pan while cooking rice
  5. Once rice is cooked, serve everything and sprinkle spring onions on top. Happy days

Special Spaghetti

Description

This special spaghetti is beyond your wildest dreams.

It's ingenius because its a concatenation of two previous recipes. No extra work required!

Ingredients

  • A prepared batch of Excellent Eggs
  • A slice of Tantalising Toast
  • One pack of spaghetti
  • A comdiment of choice, preferably cheese

Method

  1. Boil a pot of water and add the spaghetti, cooking as per packet instructions.
  2. While cooking, prepare the slice of tantalising toast as per the provided recipe
  3. Also cook up some excellent eggs
  4. Once the spaghetti is cooked, drain and combine with the toast and eggs
  5. Apply condiment of choice
  6. Merrily consume with haste!

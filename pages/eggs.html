Excellent Eggs

Description

Every time I eat eggs, I am exceptionally eurphoric!

Baked, scrambelled, poached, fried, boiled. You name it. Eggs are rad.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • A slice of Tantalising Toast
  • A comdiment of choice, preferably hot sauce

Method

  1. Cook the eggs to your desired liking.
  2. While cooking, prepare the slice of tantalising toast as per the provided recipe
  3. Once cooked, apply condiment of choice
  4. Merrily consume with haste!
